Guide: How to Eat an Apple (10 Steps)

  • Step 1: Choose Your Apple
  • Step 2: Wash Your Apple
  • Step 3: Cut Your Apple (Optional)
  • Step 4: Hold Your Apple Properly
  • Step 5: Take the First Bite
  • Step 6: Chew the Apple Properly
  • Step 7: Swallow the Apple
  • Step 8: Take Another Bite
  • Step 9: Repeat Steps 6-8
  • Step 10: Dispose of the Core

Step 1: Choose Your Apple

When choosing an apple, look for one that is firm, unbruised, and has a smooth skin. You can choose from a variety of apple types depending on your preference. Some popular types include:

  • Red Delicious
  • Gala
  • Honeycrisp
  • Granny Smith

Step 2: Wash Your Apple

Before eating your apple, be sure to wash it thoroughly under running water. This will help remove any dirt, bacteria, or pesticides that may be present on the skin. Dry your apple with a clean towel or paper towel before moving on to the next step.

Step 3: Cut Your Apple (Optional)

If you prefer to eat your apple sliced, you can cut it into pieces using a sharp knife. Be sure to remove the core and any seeds before cutting. You can also use an apple slicer to make the process easier and more efficient.

Step 4: Hold Your Apple Properly

Hold your apple with one hand, placing your thumb on one end and your four fingers on the other end. This will give you a good grip on the apple while you eat it.

Step 5: Take the First Bite

Take a bite out of the apple, making sure to get a good amount of flesh in your mouth. Don't bite

Step 6: Chew the Apple Properly

Chew the apple slowly and thoroughly, allowing the enzymes in your saliva to break down the food and aid in digestion. Swish the apple around in your mouth to make sure you get the full flavor.

Step 7: Swallow the Apple

Once you have chewed the apple properly, swallow it. Take a sip of water if necessary to help wash it down.

Step 8: Take Another Bite

Take another bite of the apple, and repeat steps 6 and 7 until you have eaten as much of the apple as you desire.

Step 9: Repeat Steps 6-8

Continue to repeat steps 6-8 until you have finished eating your apple.

Step 10: Dispose of the Core

When you have finished eating your apple, dispose of the core in a compost bin or trash can. If you are eating an apple on the go, look for a trash can to dispose of the core responsibly.
